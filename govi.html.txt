    <div class='events'>
      <p class='chrono'>The chronological life events of Darth Vader, formerly Anakin Skywalker, are outlined as follows:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><p>Birth of Anakin Skywalker (41.9 BBY): Anakin is born on the desert planet Tatooine. He is discovered by Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn, who senses strong Force potential in the young slave.</p></li>
        <li><p>Training as a Jedi (32 BBY): Anakin is trained as a Jedi under the guidance of Obi-Wan Kenobi, despite concerns about his attachment and fear.</p></li>
        <li><p>Clone Wars (22 BBY - 19 BBY): Anakin becomes a hero in the Clone Wars, displaying exceptional piloting and combat skills. He also forms a close bond with Padmé Amidala, secretly marrying her.</p></li>
        <li><p>Fall to the Dark Side (19 BBY): Driven by fear for Padmé's life and manipulated by Emperor Palpatine (Darth Sidious), Anakin succumbs to the dark side, becoming Darth Vader. He participates in the Jedi Purge, aiding in the destruction of the Jedi Order.</p></li>
        <li><p>Battle of Mustafar (19 BBY): Anakin, now Darth Vader, engages in a lightsaber duel with Obi-Wan on the volcanic planet Mustafar. He is severely injured and left for dead.</p></li>
        <li><p>Transformation into Darth Vader (19 BBY): Rescued by Palpatine, Anakin undergoes extensive cybernetic reconstruction, transforming into the iconic figure known as Darth Vader.</p></li>
        <li><p>Servitude to the Emperor (19 BBY - 4 BBY): Darth Vader becomes the enforcer of the Galactic Empire under Emperor Palpatine, hunting down remaining Jedi and enforcing imperial rule.</p></li>
        <li><p>Discovery of Luke Skywalker (0 BBY): Vader discovers that the Rebel pilot who destroyed the Death Star is his son, Luke Skywalker.</p></li>
      
        <li><p>Redemption and Death (4 ABY): In the Battle of Endor, Vader is redeemed by the love and compassion of his son, Luke. He ultimately sacrifices himself to destroy the Emperor, bringing balance to the Force.</p></li>

      </ul>
      
      <p class="sequence">This sequence summarizes the major life events of Anakin Skywalker's transformation into Darth Vader and his eventual redemption.</p>
